## AI Frontier (Use Cases)

**Karpathy's "Autoresearch" Goes Live:** Andrej Karpathy published results from letting an AI agent autonomously tune his nanochat model for ~2 days. Key findings:
- The agent made ~700 autonomous changes, finding ~20 that improved validation loss
- All improvements were additive and transferred to larger models
- "Time to GPT-2" benchmark dropped from 2.02 to 1.80 hours (~11% improvement)
