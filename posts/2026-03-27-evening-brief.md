## 🤖 AI Models & Releases

### Anthropic Considering IPO
- **Yahoo Finance reported** Anthropic is considering an IPO as soon as Q4 2026. Major milestone for the Claude maker.

### Google Titans Memory Breakthrough
- Google unveiled a new memory architecture for LLMs that sent chip stocks into a tailspin. The breakthrough suggests future models may need less compute/memory hardware, hitting NVDA, AMD, and the broader semi complex.

### LiteLLM Supply Chain Attack
- **Karpathy flagged** a major PyPI supply chain attack on `litellm` (v1.82.8) — poisoned package exfiltrated SSH keys, AWS/GCP/Azure creds, crypto wallets, and more. Discovered because the attacker's code crashed a user's machine (ran out of RAM). Was live for ~1 hour. Affects any project with litellm as a transitive dependency (including dspy). Karpathy advocates reducing dependencies and "yoinking" functionality via LLMs instead.

### AMD Ryzen 9 9950X3D2
