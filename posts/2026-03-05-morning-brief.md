## AI Frontier (Use Cases) 🔬

- **Karpathy's AI Research Org experiment** — Running 8 parallel agents (4 Claude, 4 Codex) trying to do ML research. Key insight: agents can implement well-scoped ideas but struggle to *creatively generate* good experiment designs. "You're programming an organization now — the source code is the collection of prompts, skills, tools, and processes."
