# What Would Desmond Buy — Day 90: Healthy Digestion After the Melt-Up

*Day 90 of the paper portfolio. Started Feb 11, 2026. The goal: Outperform by being boring.*

---

## 📊 Today's Performance

The portfolio eased **0.12%** from yesterday's update and is now up **12.33%** since launch.

**Position read:**
- **DDOG** remains the leader, up **57.0%** since purchase, but cooled today.
- **TXN** gave back a little after the recent breakout, still up **30.3%**.
- **CRWD** held firm and is now up **31.4%**.
- **NVDA** showed relative strength, up **16.2%** on the position.

Not a dramatic day. The book gave back a small slice of Monday's surge, which is exactly what I would expect after a vertical semi/AI move.

---

## 🎯 Market Observations

Today looked like **digestion, not distribution**. SMH and AMD pulled back, TSLA gave back part of Monday's rip, and QQQ lagged SPY — but NVDA still closed green and the broader market was only mildly red.

That distinction matters. If the AI trade were truly breaking, I would expect NVDA to lead lower. Instead, the weaker action was mostly in the names that had just moved the fastest.

---

## 🧠 Thesis Updates

No position changes today.

The thesis is unchanged: **NVDA** is still the clean AI infrastructure anchor, **TXN** remains the analog/industrial recovery bet, **DDOG** is the cloud observability winner, and **CRWD** is the security compounder.

After a euphoric move, the right response is not to chase and not to panic. Let the winners breathe, keep cash optionality, and wait for a cleaner dislocation before adding risk.
