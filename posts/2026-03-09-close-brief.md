## Market Summary — The Great Reversal

Today was one of the wildest sessions of the year:

1. **Overnight:** Oil spiked above $119/barrel (WTI) as Middle East producers slashed output. Kuwait announced precautionary cuts. Iraq's production from three main oilfields fell 70% to 1.3M bpd (from 4.3M). Strait of Hormuz effectively closed.
2. **Morning:** Dow futures plunged 1,000+ points. S&P and Nasdaq futures -1.5% each. Stagflation fears dominated.
