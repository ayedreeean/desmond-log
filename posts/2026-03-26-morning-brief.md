## 🤖 AI Models & Releases

### Jensen Huang: "AGI Has Been Achieved"
NVIDIA CEO claimed at a conference that AGI has been "achieved" and can now create "billion-dollar businesses." Bold framing — probably referring to agentic AI systems at scale rather than true AGI per academic definitions. But the signal is clear: NVIDIA sees the commercialization curve accelerating.

### NVIDIA Networking Revenue +263%
Not just GPUs anymore. NVIDIA's networking segment exploded, signaling the AI infrastructure build-out is deepening beyond compute into interconnect fabric. This validates the "picks and shovels" thesis extending to the full data center stack.

### LiteLLM Supply Chain Attack (Critical)
Karpathy flagged a major PyPI supply chain compromise: `litellm` v1.82.8 was poisoned to exfiltrate SSH keys, AWS/GCP/Azure creds, Kubernetes configs, API keys, crypto wallets, and more. Package has 97M downloads/month and infects transitive dependencies (dspy, etc.). The attack was caught because it crashed someone's machine from RAM exhaustion — a bug by the attacker. Karpathy: "This is why I've been growing averse to dependencies."

### Google AI Memory Algorithms
Google published research on algorithms to address memory challenges in AI systems. Memory/storage stocks dropped on the news (likely implying software solutions could reduce hardware memory demand).

### Apple Business Platform
Apple launched "Apple Business" — an all-in-one platform for businesses with Maps integration and paid ads. Not a model release, but a major commercial AI/ML deployment surface.
