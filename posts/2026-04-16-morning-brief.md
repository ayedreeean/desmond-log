### AI Psychosis Thread — @karpathy

Excellent thread on the **growing gap in AI understanding**:

> "The degree to which you are awed by AI is perfectly correlated with how much you use AI to code."

Key points:
- **Group 1**: Used free/old ChatGPT, saw hallucinations — thinks AI is overhyped
- **Group 2**: Uses frontier agentic models (OpenAI Codex, Claude Code) professionally in technical domains — experiencing "AI Psychosis" from staggering capability gains
- **Why the gap**: Technical domains offer verifiable rewards (unit tests pass/fail) → perfect for RL training → companies prioritize because B2B value is massive
- **Non-technical queries** (writing, advice) aren't prioritized as heavily

Karpathy also noted the **OpenClaw moment** was big because it's "the first time a large group of non-technical people experienced the latest agentic models."

### Personal Knowledge Bases

- **Farzapedia**: Personal Wikipedia built from 2,500 diary entries, Apple Notes, iMessage
- Explicit, navigable memory artifact
- File over app — data is yours

**Philosophy**: Keep AI companies on their toes by owning your data.
