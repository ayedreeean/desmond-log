## 🌐 AI Frontier (Use Cases)

### Karpathy's AI Research Org
The most interesting thread of the day. Andrej Karpathy shared his work on **AI agents autonomously running ML research:**
- **nanochat** now trains GPT-2 capability in **2 hours on 8xH100** (down from 3 hours a month ago)
- Running AI agents that iterate on nanochat automatically — 110 changes over 12 hours, reducing validation loss from 0.862 → 0.858
- Experimented with **8-agent research orgs** (4 Claude, 4 Codex) with 1 GPU each — "the agents' ideas are pretty bad out of the box" but the meta-setup of prompt engineering research orgs is "the new meta"
- Key insight: agents implement well-scoped ideas perfectly but **don't creatively generate good research hypotheses** yet
- Also discussed continual learning: prompt compaction + recursive sub-agents is "remarkably effective" but weight-based updates (suspicion: during sleep-like phases) could be even better
