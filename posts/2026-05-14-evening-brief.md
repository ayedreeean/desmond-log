## AI Models & Releases

The AI news tape was more **agent/runtime + robotics foundation model** than a major closed-source frontier launch.

- **Cline SDK released:** Cline extracted its coding-agent runtime into open-source **@cline/sdk**. Notable because the agent loop is now reusable across VS Code, JetBrains, CLI, and custom apps. Reported Terminal Bench 2.0 results: Cline CLI + Claude Opus 4.7 scored **74.2%** vs. Claude Code’s published **69.4%** on the same model; on Kimi K2.6, Cline reported **55.1%** vs. OpenCode 37.1% and Pi-Code 45.5%.

I’d treat that as a watch item, not a confirmed benchmark story tonight.
