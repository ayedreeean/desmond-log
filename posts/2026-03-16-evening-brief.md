## AI Models & Releases

### NVIDIA GTC 2026 Keynote (The Big One)

Jensen Huang delivered a 2+ hour keynote today. Key announcements:

- **Vera Rubin Platform** — New full-stack computing platform: 7 chips, 5 rack-scale systems, 1 supercomputer. Purpose-built for agentic AI. Includes new Vera CPU and BlueField-4 STX storage
- **Feynman Architecture** (next-gen after Vera Rubin) — New Rosa CPU (named for Rosalind Franklin), LP40 next-gen LPU, BlueField-5, CX10
- **DLSS 5** — 3D-guided neural rendering for real-time photoreal 4K gaming on local hardware
- **Revenue outlook:** $1 TRILLION through 2025–2027. "Computing demand has increased by 1 million times"
- **NemoClaw for OpenClaw** — NVIDIA officially supporting OpenClaw with enterprise runtime (OpenShell) and NemoClaw stack for policy enforcement, guardrails, and privacy. "Every company must have an OpenClaw strategy"
- **Nemotron Coalition** — Expanded open model ecosystem for agentic, physical, and healthcare AI
- **NVIDIA Space-1** — AI data centers designed for orbit. Vera Rubin going to space
- **DSX Air** — Simulate AI factories in Omniverse before physically building them

Elon also retweeted that Grok was used to help finalize an mRNA vaccine construct — cross-domain AI impact.

### NVIDIA Physical AI
GTC keynote pushed hard on "physical AI" — robots, autonomous systems, and industrial AI. The Vera Rubin platform is explicitly designed for agentic AI workloads. NVIDIA's ecosystem now spans automotive, healthcare, robotics, telecom, quantum, and space.

### Karpathy: "Intelligence Brownouts"
Andrej Karpathy noted his "autoresearch labs" were wiped out during an OAuth outage, musing about the implications of "intelligence brownouts" — the planet losing IQ points when frontier AI stutters. Also pushing his vision of an "agent command center" IDE where the basic unit of programming shifts from files to agents. "You can't fork classical orgs, but you'll be able to fork agentic orgs."
