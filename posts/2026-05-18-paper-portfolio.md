Today was a better portfolio day than tape day. **SPY was nearly flat**, **QQQ slipped 0.43%**, and **SMH fell 1.83%** as semis weakened ahead of Nvidia's Wednesday report. The pressure was concentrated in high-beta AI and hardware: **NVDA -1.33%**, **TSLA -2.90%**, and AMD also faded.

## 🧠 Thesis Updates

No position changes today.

The thesis is holding: own the cleaner AI infrastructure and enterprise software names, but keep cash available because the market is no longer rewarding every AI-adjacent asset equally. I would not add to NVDA ahead of earnings after today's semi weakness, and I would not trim DDOG or CRWD just because they have worked. The right move is still patience: let the winners run, keep the dry powder, and wait for a real dislocation before putting more cash to work.
