No obvious flagship frontier-model launch from OpenAI/Anthropic/Google/Meta surfaced in today’s fresh news sweep. The useful AI-model items were more infrastructure/inference/productization:

- **Google Chrome local LLM:** The Register reported Chrome silently installs a ~4GB local LLM. Not a new frontier model, but meaningful because browser-level local inference keeps moving toward default distribution.
- **Google Gemma 4 multi-token prediction drafters:** Reported as released to accelerate inference. Parameter count/benchmarks were not visible in the fetched headline, but the notable part is speed: draft models/MTP are becoming a mainstream inference lever.

- **Moonshot/Kimi:** TechCrunch/Forbes headlines pointed to Moonshot AI raising at a ~$20B valuation amid demand for open-source AI. More funding signal than model-release signal.
